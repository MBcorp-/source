A text-to-speech engine based on phoneme synthesis. Because there is no direct access to audio hardware on a non-rooted Android device, this utility requires the assistance of android-audio-sink to capture and play the audio stream. The scripts /usr/bin/say and /usr/bin/sayfile demonstrate how to pipe the audio data into android-audio-sink. Note that android-audio-sink is not stated as a dependency, because espeak will work without it, for (say) writing the audio data to a file.
