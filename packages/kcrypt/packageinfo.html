kcrypt is a file encryption and obfuscation utility, originally written twenty years ago for Windows. It can operate on a directory full of files, and mangle their filenames as well as encrypting them. Original filenames are restored on decryption.

On Linux, such operations are usually better handled using encrypted filesystems. However, I have resurrected the utility for KBOX, because non-rooted devices don't have access to encrypted filesystems.
